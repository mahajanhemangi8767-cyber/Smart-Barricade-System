# NIYANTRAK – Smart Barricade System for Dynamic Crowd Control

## Problem Statement

Managing large crowds during mega-events such as Kumbh Mela, Ganesh festivals, concerts, IPL matches, rallies, protests, and emergency evacuations is a major challenge in India.

Traditional barricades are:

- Static and non-adaptive
- Completely manpower-dependent
- Unable to detect crowd density in real time
- Difficult to reposition quickly during emergencies
- Inefficient in preventing congestion and stampedes

Because of this, authorities face problems such as:

- Crowd congestion and bottlenecks
- Stampedes and panic situations
- Delayed emergency response
- Poor accessibility for elderly and disabled people
- High operational and manpower costs

There is currently no intelligent, automated, and scalable system in India that can dynamically monitor crowd movement and assist authorities in real-time crowd management.

---

# Proposed Solution

We propose **NIYANTRAK**, an AI-powered Smart Barricade System that transforms traditional barricades into intelligent and adaptive crowd management units.

The system uses:

- AI-based crowd analysis
- Real-time monitoring
- Predictive risk detection
- Automated decision support
- Smart dashboards and alerts

NIYANTRAK continuously analyzes crowd flow using sensors and computer vision, predicts congestion or dangerous crowd surges, and helps authorities manage movement efficiently.

The barricade system can:

- Detect overcrowded zones
- Predict stampede risks
- Provide multilingual alerts
- Suggest optimal barricade placement
- Monitor emergency evacuation routes
- Reduce manpower requirements

The solution is scalable, sustainable, modular, and suitable for smart cities and large-scale public events.

# Working Methodology

## Step 1 – Deployment & Calibration
Barricades are deployed at event locations and calibrated.

## Step 2 – Live Data Collection
Sensors and cameras continuously capture crowd movement and density.

## Step 3 – AI-Based Analysis
AI models analyze congestion, bottlenecks, and crowd behavior.

## Step 4 – Prediction & Decision
The system predicts risks and recommends barricade adjustments.

## Step 5 – Alert Generation
Authorities and the public receive instant alerts through dashboard, SMS, and audio systems.

## Step 6 – Monitoring & Reporting
All activities are tracked on a central dashboard with analytics and reports.
